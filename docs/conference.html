About INTAC Conference

The International Tourism & Cultures Conference (INTAC) is the premier platform that brings together tourism professionals, cultural experts, government representatives, and industry leaders from around the globe. Our 2026 edition focuses on transformative strategies for sustainable tourism development, digital innovation, and cultural preservation.

Join us for three days of insightful discussions, networking opportunities, and knowledge exchange that will shape the future of tourism and cultural exchange worldwide.
