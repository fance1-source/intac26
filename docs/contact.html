Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I register as a visitor?

You can register as a visitor through our online registration portal on the website. Registration is free and gives you access to all exhibition areas. Simply fill out the visitor registration form with your details.

What are the exhibition hours?

The exhibition is open from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM daily during the event dates. Conference sessions may have different timings, so please check the conference program for specific session times.

How can my company become an exhibitor?

To become an exhibitor, please contact our sales team at exhibitors@intac.my or call +603 4141 5511. Our team will guide you through the exhibition package options and booking process.

Is there parking available at the venue?

Yes, the World Trade Centre Kuala Lumpur has ample parking facilities. There are both indoor and outdoor parking options available. We recommend arriving early during peak hours as parking can get busy.
