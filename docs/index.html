About INTAC'26

International Tourism, Arts & Culture Exhibition & Conference

INTAC'26 is the premier global platform dedicated to fostering connections and celebrating the rich tapestry of **International Tourism, Arts & Culture**. Our exhibition and conference bring together industry leaders, policymakers, artists, and travelers to explore new frontiers in sustainable tourism, cultural preservation, and creative exchange. Join us to experience a world of opportunities and inspiration.

  • Global Networking Opportunities
  • Showcase of Diverse Arts & Cultures
  • Focus on Sustainable Tourism Practices
  • High-Level Conference & Keynote Speakers
